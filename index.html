"What's In It For Me?"

Gain confidence and understanding of the financial tools and strategies that will help you:

01

Evaluate Your Financial Team

Know what to be asking for, how to use it, and who should be able to provide it for you. Finish with a clear framework for assessing whether your current CFO, or financial lead, is truly capable of delivering strategic information.

02

About the Workshop

An Exclusive Vistage Member Workshop

Turn On The Headlights is a hands-on, interactive workshop designed specifically for Vistage Chairs, CE groups and members, and aligned Key groups. It is presented primarily through the Vistage Speaker Bureau or directly by Scott Crawford and Bob McDonnell.

The seminar is built on a simple but powerful idea: most CEOs are driving their business by unknowingly looking in the rearview mirror — at historical financials, last quarter's numbers, last year's results. Turn On The Headlights is designed to change that. It helps CEOs develop a forward-looking mindset, and equips them with the right forward-looking tools and the right financial team to illuminate and navigate the road ahead.
